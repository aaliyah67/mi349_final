Projects

During my time at MSU, I have worked on a variety of projects related to cybersecurity and IT.

Research Proposal on Social Media and Cybersecurity

This project was a research Proposal about the effects of social media on cybersecurity. Through this proposal, I identified how social media can create cybersecurity vulnerabilities. I then proposed a solution of creating tools to educate users, resulting in less vulnerabilities.

Cloud Networking Project

For this project, I worked with a team of 4 to design a company that utilizes cloud networking tools. For the first part of this project, we designed a full website for our company. Then, we utilized Amazon Web Services (AWS) to successfully deploy and host our website.

Research Paper on the Relationship Between the IoT and Cybersecurity

For this project, I did research on the relationship between the Internet of Things (IoT) and Cybersecurity. Through this research, I found that many users of the IoT and IoT devices are not aware of how their data is stored, and how to protect their data. I discovered that there is a correlation with user awareness and cybersecurity vulnerabilities.
